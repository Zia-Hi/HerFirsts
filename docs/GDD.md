---

# Controls

Mouse movement

Left Click

Drag

Simple object interactions

No combat

No inventory management complexity

---

# Inventory

Minimal.

Tools only.

Appears on the right side.

Inspired by Granny.

Objects can be picked up and put down.

---

# Failure System

Failure is reversible.

Examples:

Touch electrical wire without gloves.

↓

Screen briefly fades black.

↓

Player returns to previous safe state.

↓

Receives explanation.

Three failures:

Mission restarts.

No punishment beyond retrying.

---

# Knowledge Card System

Every completed mission unlocks:

Problem

Cause

Required Tools

Safety Notes

Repair Steps

Tips

Knowledge Cards remain permanently accessible.

The game should reward curiosity.
